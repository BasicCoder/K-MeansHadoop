.ngo, .ong and the OnGood community website are now available to NGOs.

Introducing OnGood™

We've unveiled the name of the new online community for NGOs, nonprofits and charities: OnGood! Available in early 2015, OnGood will be a versatile online tool to help garner greater visibility and raise funds for NGOs. Read more

Build trust in your organisation with .ngo

Introducing .ngo and .ong, an online community open only to genuine non-governmental organisations. The first step to registering a .ngo domain is to submit your Expression of Interest (EOI). It’s quick, simple and free. Find out more
